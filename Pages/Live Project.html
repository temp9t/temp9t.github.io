Case Study Of Live Project

This is a Case Study of Silly Mittens Cakery.
Silly Mittens Cakery is a small business operated by Chef Anamika Biswas. She currently operates everthing from home. Her shop offers all diffenrent types of cakes and sweets. She also take Party oders and Customize cake orders. The goal of the project was to develop a simple website where she can display everthing about her business. Following you will find all the information regarding this project. This website checked-out all her needs.


Business Goal

To develop a website to increase the Brand name of Silly Mittens Cakery and to display all the offerings from cake shop.

Usability Goal

Make a simple website for user to know all the menu and "what's new" to offer from silly Mittens Cakery. To make it easy to contact and ask for information.
